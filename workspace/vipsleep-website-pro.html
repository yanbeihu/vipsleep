How does the 100-night trial work?

Is the pillow cover washable?
Yes! The removable cover is machine washable. Simply unzip, remove, and wash in cold water. Air dry or tumble dry on low heat for best results.

Our standard size is 26" x 17" (66cm x 43cm), perfect for standard pillowcases. The queen size is 30" x 20" (76cm x 51cm).

With proper care, your VIPSLEEP pillow should last 5-7 years. We include a 5-year warranty to give you peace of mind.

Absolutely! The adjustable fill allows you to customize the height and firmness to your preferred sleep position – side, back, or stomach.
